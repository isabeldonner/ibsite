What is the IB Program?

IB stands for International Baccalaureate. The high school IB program is comprised of six core subjects along with the DP core, comprising theory of knowledge (TOK), creativity, activity, service (CAS) and the extended essay. IB classes are more advanced than typical classes.

Requirements to be in the IB Program Here at Gables

  • A minimum of 2.5 Grade Point Average in each core academic subject area for the previous year and the first grading period of the current year combined.
  • A minimum of 2.0 Grade Point Average in conduct for the core academic subject areas.
  • All effort grades in core academic classes must be a “2”.
  • No more than 10 unexcused absences for the previous year and 5 unexcused absences for the first semester of the current year.
  • Physical Science Honors and Algebra I Honors be completed and credit received before the 2017-18 school year begins.

What's it Like Being Part of this Program?

Being in IB takes dedication and it is rigorous. However, if you are committed and stick with IB, you will receive great benefits! In this program, students are given more independence.

Why Should You Join the IB Program?

  • Allows you to take college classes in high school!
  • If you take college classes now, you don't have to pay for them later!
  • Prepares you for life and academics later on!
  • Being part of the IB program looks good on college applications!
  • IB credits are recognized my most colleges all around the world!
  • The list goes on!
